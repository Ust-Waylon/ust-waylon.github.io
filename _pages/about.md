Nice to meet you! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk), supervised by [Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing WONG](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~raywong/). I obtained a BSc degree in Data Science and Technology (DSCT) at HKUST in 2024.

My research focuses on **data mining** and **machine learning**, with an emphasis on **session-based recommendation**. I am also interested in how large language models can advance recommendation systems.
